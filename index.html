Welcome to the world of The GirlyPops! We are five dedicated Digital Media students from UCF, and we created this brand to deliver the ultimate Y2K Pop aesthetic of your dreams.

Our shared passion for collectibles and pop culture led us straight to the idea of a blind box brand. We blended our love for high-energy 'pop girl groups' (like KATSEYE!) with our own personal obsession with Popmart blind boxes, creating a true best-of-both-worlds scenario for collectors.

Our team is our own 'Girl Group': Chaney handled project management, Nadia brought the art to life, Anh managed the programming, and Ella & Gabi perfected the design. We hope you enjoy exploring The GirlyPops and fall in love with our characters as much as we have!
