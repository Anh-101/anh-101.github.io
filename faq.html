FAQ

Quick answers about GirlyPops, blind boxes, orders, and drops. If you don’t see your question, contact us.

Blind-Box Basics

How do GirlyPops blind boxes work?
Each sealed box includes one GirlyPops character at random from the current drop. Distribution is even, so every character has the same chance.
What comes in a box?
One character item plus a mini info card. Some drops add a sticker or printable; the product page will say if so.

Orders & Shipping

Where do you ship?
We currently ship within the U.S. and select locations. Available options and rates are shown at checkout.
How long does shipping take?
Orders usually ship within 3–5 business days after fulfillment. Delivery times depend on the carrier and your location.
Can I change my address after ordering?
If your order hasn’t shipped yet, reach out via Contact with your order number and the updated address.
My package says delivered but it isn’t here.
Check with household members and nearby units first. If it doesn’t appear within 2 days, message us through Contact with your order number.

Restocks & Drops

When is the next drop?
We announce drop dates on our site. Watch the Shop page banners for the latest timing.
Do you restock sold-out items?
Sometimes. If a restock is planned, we’ll note it on the product page with a date or window.
Can I preorder?
If a drop supports preorders, the product page will clearly show “Preorder” and the estimated ship window.

Returns & Exchanges

What’s your return policy?
Unopened items can be returned within 14 days of delivery. Start a request on the Contact page with your order number.
My item arrived damaged—what now?
Send a brief note and a photo via Contact within 7 days of delivery and we’ll sort it out.
Can I exchange for a specific character?
We can’t swap for exact characters, but you’re welcome to trade with friends/collectors.

Community

Can I send fan art?
Yes! Share it with us through Contact. We love featuring community art (with credit).
Do you do collabs?
We’re open to small collabs. Pitch the idea with links to your work via Contact.

Still have a question? Contact us and we’ll get back to you.
